Services

Operational leadership and pre-opening support for Miami hospitality.

Each service is built around a different moment: launch, stabilization, recovery, or system building. The goal is always the same - calm operations, clear standards, and a guest experience that feels intentional.

Fractional F&B Operations Director

Monthly fractional leadership for operators who need senior support without a full-time salary. I plug into the existing team and focus on what guests and P&L feel first.

Best for restaurants, hotel groups, and multi-concept operators needing senior leadership without a full-time executive.

  • Weekly on-site operational leadership during key services.
  • P&L review and cost-structure optimization.
  • Labor systems and productivity standards.
  • Vendor review and manager coaching.

Investment: Starting at $3,500/month.

Pre-Opening Consulting

Project-based support for new builds, hotel F&B launches, and concept rebrands within six months of opening.

Best for teams moving from construction and design into training, soft opening, and launch.

  • Hiring timeline and org chart.
  • Vendor sourcing and SOP creation.
  • Training plan and soft-opening execution.
  • 30/60/90-day post-opening audits.

Investment: Starting at $8,500 per project.

Operations Recovery & Restructuring

A 30-90 day intensive for underperforming locations, leadership transitions, or post-acquisition stabilization.

Best for properties where guest experience and team confidence have slipped and need to be reset.

  • Three-day operational diagnostic.
  • Written action plan with priorities and timelines.
  • SOP rewrites and retraining where needed.
  • Guest experience recovery strategy.

Investment: Starting at $5,500.

SOP & Training System Development

Documentation and training systems designed to outlast any one manager. Built for operators who need consistency across teams and locations.

Best for multi-location operators or teams that rely heavily on strong supervisors.

  • Custom manuals for FOH, BOH, banquet, and catering.
  • Opening and closing checklists.
  • Pre-shift templates and service scripts.
  • Leadership handoff training and documentation.

Investment: Starting at $2,500 per system.

Miami-based and strongest on-site in South Florida, with hybrid support available for planning and SOP work. Fractional engagements often begin within 2-3 weeks; pre-opening support should start 4-6 months before launch.
