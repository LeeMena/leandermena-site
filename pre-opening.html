Pre-Opening Consulting by Leander Mena

Leander Mena has led pre-opening operations for Miami restaurants, hotel F&B programs, and banquet facilities. Engagements typically begin 90–120 days before opening and run through the first 30–60 days of operation.

90–60 days out

Foundation Phase

Vendor sourcing and contract review, menu and service concept development, kitchen and FOH workflow design, initial SOP drafting, hiring plan and org chart.

60–30 days out

Build Phase

Staff recruitment and onboarding, training program delivery, POS and systems setup, health department preparation, dry-run service execution.

30 days out — opening

Launch Phase

Soft opening management, daily operational debriefs, real-time SOP adjustment, ownership reporting, team performance evaluation.

Post-opening

Stabilization Support

Optional 30–60 day post-opening support to transition operations to the permanent management team with documented handoff materials.
