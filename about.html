About Leander Mena

Leander Mena is a Miami-based hospitality and food-and-beverage operations professional with more than 18 years of hands-on experience managing restaurant, hotel, banquet, and catering operations across South Florida.

Career & Background

Over nearly two decades, Leander Mena has built a career defined by operational leadership at some of Miami's most demanding hospitality venues. From independent restaurants and boutique hotels to large-scale banquet facilities and luxury resort properties, Leander has led teams, built systems, and delivered consistent guest experiences at every scale.

Leander's career spans front-of-house management, pre-opening builds, banquet operations, and fractional consulting engagements across the Miami and South Florida market. He has served as Opening General Manager, Director of Banquets, and General Manager at multiple high-profile concepts, giving him deep fluency in both independent and branded hospitality environments.

Notable roles include Opening General Manager at Maska Indian Kitchen + Bar — a Miami concept led by a Michelin-starred chef — Director of Banquets (Pre-Opening) at SLS Brickell, and General Manager at Marabu Restaurant. Each role required building operational frameworks from scratch or restructuring underperforming teams.

Professional Philosophy

Leander believes that great hospitality operations are built on three pillars: clear systems, well-trained people, and relentless follow-through. Whether he is opening a new concept, stabilizing an existing one, or leading a fractional engagement, his focus is always on creating the operational backbone that allows a team to perform consistently without constant supervision.

His approach to leadership is direct and collaborative. He works alongside ownership and management teams, not above them, to build the kind of operational culture that retains staff and earns guest loyalty over time.
